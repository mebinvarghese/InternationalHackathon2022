Shadowing at our Hackathon

What is the role
of a shadow?

The role of a shadow is to follow around a certain group of people in order to gain real-life, practical experience by observing their activities and aiding them to carry out duties.

A perfect example of such training would be medical students accompanying their senior doctors and peers during patient visits.

What will be the experience like?

Your role as a shadow will commence on the 31st of January 2022 - one week prior to the actual event. We will invite you to attend an Induction at our HQ on your first day. This induction will give you an in-depth understanding of all the tools, technologies and services we will be using during the event, as well as, help you set up for your day to day activities in the office. We will also give you a tour of the venue and introduce you to the ITS events team and all the key people you will be interacting with during the event.

We will provide you with all the tools and software required to efficiently carry out your duties and responsibilities. However, you will be required to bring your own laptop.

What training
will I recieve?

You will receive intensive training in the areas of project management, event planning, web/app development people management, hospitality, marketing, sales and business development etc.

Shadows who showcase outstanding performance in their role may also be eligible to become Ambassadors of Irish Tech Society.
