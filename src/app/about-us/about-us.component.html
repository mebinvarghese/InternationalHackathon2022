About Irish Tech Society

A non-profit, collaborative community platform accessible to anyone anywhere in the global tech sector.

Irish Tech Society is a unique, non-profit organisation. Whilst recognising the power of technology in transforming our lives and our society, we focus on the people who innovate, develop, and ultimately benefit from those technologies.

Our mission is to offer leadership, guidance, continuous learning and professional development opportunities to all individuals in the tech sector worldwide.

We do this while simultaneously promoting Ireland as a global tech capital. Due to its highly collaborative tech ecosystem, higher standards of education, geographical advantage as the closest market access point between the US, Canada, UK and EU, Ireland already has a special place in the global tech sector.
